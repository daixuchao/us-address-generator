Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 13, 2026

Global Address Generator is a static web tool. The address generation features run in the browser and do not require an account, payment information, or submitted personal profile data.

Generated data

Address, name, and phone number examples produced by this site are synthetic test data. They are generated for display, copying, and CSV export in your browser.

Hosting and basic logs

The site is hosted on Cloudflare Pages. Cloudflare may process basic request information such as IP address, browser type, request path, and timestamp for security, performance, analytics, and troubleshooting.

Google AdSense and cookies

This site includes Google AdSense advertising code. Google and its partners may use cookies, web beacons, or similar technologies to serve, limit, personalize, and measure ads. When a page uses Google services, the browser may send information such as the page URL, IP address, browser details, and device information to Google. See how Google uses information from partner sites for more details.

Advertising choices

You can review or change ad personalization settings in Google My Ad Center. You can also delete or restrict cookies through your browser settings. Where consent is required, this site may present a privacy choices message provided by Google or another Google-certified consent platform.

Local generation and storage

Address, name, and phone examples are randomly assembled by scripts running in the browser. The site does not ask users to submit generated results and does not maintain a user address-history database. Copy and CSV export actions take place on the user's device.
