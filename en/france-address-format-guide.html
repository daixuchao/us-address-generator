France Address Format Guide

French addresses commonly use recipient, street line, postal code, city, and country. Apartment, building, staircase, floor, and care-of details may appear as additional lines.

Common layout

Camille Martin
12 Rue de Rivoli
75001 Paris
France

Fields to support

  • Recipient: person, company, or attention line.
  • Street line: number followed by street name.
  • Additional detail: apartment, building, floor, or entrance when needed.
  • Postal code and city: five-digit postal code before the city.
  • Country: France.

QA notes

Test accented characters, apostrophes, long street names, optional secondary lines, and five-digit postal-code validation. Do not require a U.S.-style state field.
