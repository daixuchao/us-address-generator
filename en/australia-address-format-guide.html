Australia Address Format Guide

Australian addresses commonly use suburb, state or territory abbreviation, and a four-digit postcode. This can break forms that expect a city/state/ZIP pattern.

Common layout

Sophie Wilson
Unit 8, 45 Collins Street
Melbourne VIC 3000
Australia

Fields to support

  • Unit or level: optional apartment, suite, level, or unit detail.
  • Street address: street number, name, and type.
  • Suburb: locality field often shown before the state code.
  • State or territory: NSW, VIC, QLD, WA, SA, TAS, ACT, or NT.
  • Postcode: four digits.

QA notes

Test optional unit labels, long suburb names, four-digit postcodes with leading or repeated digits, and state abbreviation validation. Avoid labeling every locality field as "city" for Australian users.
