Germany Address Format Guide

German addresses usually place the street name and house number on one line, followed by a five-digit postal code and city. Address forms should allow local street names and non-English characters.

Common layout

Lena Schmidt
Friedrichstrasse 82
10117 Berlin
Germany

Fields to support

  • Name: person or company name.
  • Street and house number: commonly one line, often with the number after the street.
  • Postal code: five digits.
  • City: locality such as Berlin, Munich, Hamburg, or Frankfurt am Main.
  • Country: Germany or Deutschland depending on the interface language.

QA notes

Test umlauts, long compound street names, apartment details, and postal-code validation that accepts exactly five digits without forcing U.S. state fields.
